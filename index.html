Clean Energy Born From African Soil

ASTAAN PLUG IN ECO SOLUTIONS was founded in 2025 with a singular vision — to accelerate Rwanda and East Africa's transition to clean, affordable, and reliable renewable energy. We believe every household, school, and business deserves access to sustainable power.

Rooted in Rwandan innovation and Pan-African ambition, we deliver solar, biogas, and energy-efficiency solutions that reduce carbon footprints while growing local economies.
